## The Problem: GameFi 1.0

Early decentralized gaming treated reward tokens as play-to-earn salaries. Players optimized for immediate cash-outs, creating extraction-first behavior, hyperinflation, liquidity drainage, and economic collapse. Salary-token models are structurally unsustainable.

---

## The Solution: GameFi 2.0

Terra-Forge: The Deep Core introduces an industrial mining economy on the Neoxa L1 blockchain where on-chain value and in-game utility are deliberately separated, deflationary sinks remove NMG from circulation, and every participant class has a designed path to success.

### Seven Design Pillars

1. **Separated asset classes** — NMG lives on-chain; Gold, Miners, Ores, and Ingots live in-game. Casual players are shielded from crypto volatility through Gold.

2. **Fixed 21M cap with Reserve accounting** — All smelting rewards come from a pre-allocated Smelting Reward Reserve (8,400,000 NMG). The protocol never mints beyond the hard cap.

3. **Dynamic emission scaling** — As the Reserve depletes over years, rewards scale from 1.0× down to 0.75×, 0.50×, and eventually pause below 5% — preventing cliff collapse.

4. **Irreversible deflationary sinks** — One-way NMG→Gold deposits, catalyst burns (95% on-chain), and upgrade burns (80% on-chain) continuously remove NMG from circulation.

5. **Floating NMG/NEOX market** — No peg. NMG and NEOX trade at market price on neoxa.exchange. Hard pegging would enable arbitrage against net-positive smelting economics.

6. **Everyone winnable** — Free players start with 500 Gold and 3 subsidized Bronze smelts. Capital providers earn rental income. LPs earn DEX fees. Smartnode operators earn block rewards.

7. **No NFTs** — Guild Miners are in-game database assets. The game does not use NFTs or on-chain collectibles.

## Economic Flywheel

```
Players deposit NMG → receive Gold (one-way)
       ↓
Hire miners → mine ores → smelt ingots
       ↓
Catalyst burn (95% destroyed, 5% recycled to Reserve)
       ↓
Scaled NMG reward from Reserve → optional withdrawal → neoxa.exchange
       ↓
Buyback floor protects industrial profitability
```
