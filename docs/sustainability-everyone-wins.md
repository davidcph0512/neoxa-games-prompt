## Part 2: Free Player Journey (Tutorial Catalyst Subsidy)

Every new Guild Master can complete the full gameplay loop without a wallet:

1. **Create account** → receive **500 Starter Gold**
2. **Hire Common Biotech Miner** → **200 Gold**
3. **Dispatch to Surface** → mine ~19 hours for first Bronze materials
4. **Smelt Bronze** → use **Tutorial Subsidy** (0 NMG cost, up to **3 times**)
5. **Earn ~3.6 NMG** in-game (3 × 1.20 base reward, subject to emission scaling)
6. **Continue winning** via P2P ore sales (Gold), miner rental (ore share), or optional NMG deposit
7. **Optional withdrawal** → connect wallet, wait 24h, withdraw (min 1 NMG, cap 100/day)

The Tutorial Subsidy Pool (50,000 NMG cap) funds catalyst burns so F2P players never hit the "need 0.10 NMG to smelt" wall.

## Part 3: Dynamic Reserve Emission Scaling

The Smelting Reward Reserve holds 8,400,000 NMG — finite by design. Without scaling, heavy smelting would deplete it and collapse rewards overnight.

**Effective_Reward = Base_Reward × Emission_Multiplier**

| Reserve Remaining | Multiplier | Player Message |
|-------------------|------------|----------------|
| 100% – 50% | 1.0× | Normal operations |
| 50% – 20% | 0.75× | Industrial output adjusted |
| 20% – 5% | 0.50× | Reserve conservation mode |
| Below 5% | Pause | Awaiting governance replenishment |

**Replenishment sources:**
- 5% of every catalyst burn recycles to Reserve (95% burned on-chain)
- Governance may transfer up to 10% of Game Treasury per quarter

## Part 4: Everyone Winnable — Win Matrix

### Active Player (F2P)
**Wins:** Gold wealth, guild rank, ore trading profits, ~3.6 tutorial NMG.  
**Crypto required:** None.  
**Path:** Starter Gold → mine → subsidized smelt → sell ores on P2P marketplace.

### Active Player (Invested)
**Wins:** Scaled NMG smelting rewards, wallet withdrawal, higher-tier ingots.  
**Crypto required:** Optional NMG deposit at 1:250 Gold.  
**Path:** Deposit → upgrade drill → Deep Core zones → Iron/Mithril/Astral ingots.

### Capital Provider
**Wins:** Passive ore income from rental splits (e.g., 60% owner / 40% renter).  
**Crypto required:** 50–200 NMG for elite Cyborg miners.  
**Path:** Purchase miner → list on rental marketplace → smelt or sell ore share.

### Liquidity Provider
**Wins:** 1.5% of every NMG/NEOX swap on neoxa.exchange.  
**Crypto required:** NMG/NEOX LP stake.  
**Path:** Independent of game — wins from trading volume.

### Smartnode Operator
**Wins:** 45% of L1 block rewards.  
**Crypto required:** 1,000,000 NEOX stake (~$80 USDC reference).  
**Path:** Host game DB, matchmaking, anti-cheat — secures all player state.

**Key message: Everyone can win differently.**

## FAQ

**Can I play for free?**  
Yes. Every account receives 500 Starter Gold and 3 free Bronze smelts. No wallet required.

**Will rewards run out?**  
The 8.4M NMG Reserve is finite. Emission scaling reduces rewards gradually over years. Catalyst recycling and governance replenishment extend runway. Rewards pause below 5% Reserve until replenished.

**Is NMG pegged to NEOX?**  
No. NMG/NEOX trades at a floating market price on neoxa.exchange. A hard peg would break the economy through arbitrage against smelting profits.

**Are there NFTs?**  
No. Guild Miners are in-game database assets only.

**How do I withdraw?**  
Earn NMG through smelting, connect a Neoxa L1 wallet, wait 24 hours (new accounts), then withdraw (min 1 NMG, max 100/day).
