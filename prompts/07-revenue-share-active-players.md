Act as a Game Designer documenting the Active Player economy for **Terra-Forge: The Deep Core**.

## Player Profile

**Active Players — Time-Rich, Capital-Poor**

## F2P Entry Path (No Wallet Required)

1. Create account → **500 Starter Gold** (one-time).
2. Hire Common Biotech Miner → **200 Gold**.
3. Dispatch to Surface, mine ores (~19h for first Bronze materials).
4. Smelt Bronze using **Tutorial Catalyst Subsidy** (3 free smelts, 0 NMG cost).
5. Earn **~3.6 NMG** in-game (3 × 1.20 base reward, subject to emission scaling).
6. Win Gold via **P2P ore sales** (2% marketplace fee) — Gold is the F2P win layer.
7. Optional: connect wallet, deposit NMG (1:250 Gold), withdraw after 24h cooldown.

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Ingot Smelting & Withdrawal
After tutorial subsidies: smelt with own catalyst → scaled NMG from Reserve → withdraw (min 1, cap 100/day).

### 2. P2P Ore Trading (F2P Primary Win)
Sell Coal, Iron, Copper for Gold on guild marketplace. Build wealth without ever touching crypto.

### 3. Miner Rental (Renter Side)
Rent elite miners via **server-enforced escrow** (NOT trustless). Example: 40% Renter / 60% Owner ore split.

## Gold as F2P Win Layer

F2P players win via: leaderboard rank, guild standing, marketplace Gold, ore trading profits, rental ore share, progression (Rare miner 800 Gold). NMG is the **withdrawal layer**, not the only win.
