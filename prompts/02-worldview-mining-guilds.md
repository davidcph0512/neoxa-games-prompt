# Prompt: Worldview & Narrative — The Mining Guilds (Section 1.2)

Act as a Lead Game Designer and Narrative Writer for **Terra-Forge: The Deep Core**.

**Important:** English only. No NFTs. Guild Miners are in-game industrial units. Canonical spec: `.whitepaper` v2.2.0.

## Player Identity

The player is a **Guild Master** — not a physical laborer. They operate orbital command ships and manage industrial mining operations remotely from above Planetary Core 9-C.

## Core Gameplay Fantasy

1. Deploy **Biotech Miners** and heavily shielded **Cyborg MINERs** into extreme gravitational stress zones of the deep core.
2. Extract raw ore and protect it from radiation damage during transport.
3. Process ore through high-temperature **orbital foundries** using precise physical recipes.
4. Synthesize high-purity **NMG-Ingots** and trade them to the **Interstellar Merchants Coalition** for liquid external assets.

## Asset Roles in the Narrative

- **Biotech Miners:** Organic-adapted units suited for moderate-depth extraction.
- **Cyborg MINERs:** Heavily shielded units built for deep core, high-radiation zones.
- **Raw Ores:** Iron, Copper, Coal, and Star-Element extracted from crust fractures.
- **Orbital Foundries:** High-temperature smelting stations operating in orbit above the planet.
- **NMG-Ingots:** Proof-of-value refined products that serve as redemption keys for liquid NMG rewards.
- **Interstellar Merchants Coalition:** External trade partners who purchase NMG-Ingots for real market value.

## Your Task

Design narrative systems and written content that bring the Guild Master experience to life.

## Content to Produce

1. Guild Master onboarding dialogue explaining the command-ship role and first steps.
2. Miner type descriptions covering Biotech vs Cyborg units — their lore, visual identity, and operational differences.
3. Foundry operation narrative describing what happens visually and atmospherically when ores are smelted.
4. Interstellar Merchants Coalition lore — who they are, why they buy NMG-Ingots, and what they do with them.
5. Failure-state narratives for radiation damage, fatigue collapse, and ore spoilage.
6. Achievement and milestone titles such as "Deep Core Prospector," "Ingot Baron," and "Guild Master of 9-C."
7. In-game tooltip texts for core UI elements: miner dispatch, recall, forge, and redeem actions.

## Tone

- Command-and-control military-industrial aesthetic
- The player should feel like the CEO of a mining conglomerate, not a pickaxe wielder
- Emphasis on logistics, scheduling, and industrial optimization

## Tutorial Opening Line

"Welcome, Guild Master. To begin your descent into Planetary Core 9-C, convert your first NMG into Gold fuel, hire a Miner, and dispatch them to the depths."
