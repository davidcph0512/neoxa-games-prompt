# Prompt: Worldview & Narrative — The Abyssal Descent (Section 1.1)

Act as a Lead Narrative Designer and Creative Director for **Terra-Forge: The Deep Core**.

**Important:** All output must be in English only. No NFTs. Canonical spec: `.whitepaper` v2.2.0.

## Setting

- **Year:** 2382
- **Location:** Planetary Core 9-C — a rogue geological body detected by deep space exploration arrays
- **Environment:** The Obsidian Crust — a cold planet with no atmosphere and no stellar orbit, composed entirely of highly compressed tectonic plates wrapped around a volatile, radioactive interior

## Lore Foundation

Within the hyper-pressurized fractures of the Obsidian Crust, friction and cosmic radiation have fused heavy metals into super-dense geode deposits. These deposits are highly enriched with **NMG (Neox Miner Guild) crystals** — a sub-space particulate matter capable of acting as a catalyst for near-instantaneous atomic fusing.

## Your Task

Create immersive narrative content that establishes the world, atmosphere, and stakes of Terra-Forge.

## Content to Produce

1. An opening cinematic script (2–3 minutes) describing the discovery of Planetary Core 9-C and the first prospector expeditions into the Obsidian Crust.
2. Environmental descriptions for multiple mining zone depth levels: surface crust, mid-fracture zones, and the deep core.
3. Lore entries explaining why NMG crystals form only under extreme gravitational stress and cosmic radiation.
4. In-game journal entries that unlock as players progress deeper into the crust.
5. Marketing copy for the landing page hero section and social media teasers.

## Tone and Style

- Industrial, gritty sci-fi with steampunk undertones
- Dark, oppressive atmosphere reflecting extreme mining conditions
- Professional prospector and mining guild terminology
- A sense of danger, frontier exploration, and hard-won reward

## Key Terms (Use Consistently)

- Planetary Core 9-C
- The Obsidian Crust
- NMG crystals / Neox Miner Guild
- Super-dense geode deposits
- Hyper-pressurized fractures
